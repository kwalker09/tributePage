Bruce Lee

Founder of Jeet Kune Do, one of the most influential martial artists of all time.

Bruce Lee takes his stance in "Enter the Dragon", which is considered one of the best martial arts movies of all time.

Here's a list of random facts.

  • His birth name is Lee Jun-fan
  • His nickname growing up in Hong Kong was King Gorilla.
  • He developed Jeet Kune Do from Wing Chun and other martial arts.
  • Uma Thurman’s outfit in Kill Bill 1 was modelled on Bruce Lee’s from his final film Game of Death.
  • Lee was born in the zodiac hour of the dragon in the year of the dragon.
  • Jackie Chan said after Lee hit him with a stick on set he exaggerated the injury so that Lee would continue to hold him to make him feel better.
  • Lee was posthumously given Beijing’s Chinese Wushu Association’s first award for achievement in November 1998.
  • Chuck Norris said Bruce Lee was the one person he couldn’t beat in a fight.
  • Bruce was so fast that often, movie directors would ask him to slow down because they could not capture his body movements clearly on film.
  • Bruce Lee’s death is still the subject of speculation, with many causes suggested including poisoning and paranormal intervention.
